NMC-No Movement Clause A player with a NMC cannot be traded or sent to a minor league team without their consent.
NTC-No Trade Clause. A Player with a NTC submits a list of 10-20 teams they would not accept a trade to. However, they can be traded to an unlisted team at any time.
UFA-Unrestricted Free Agent. A UFA is free to sign with any team.
RFA-Restricted Free Agent. A RFA can sign offer sheets with any team, but their original team has the right to match that offer sheet and retain the player.
EL or ELC-Entry Level Contract. Special contract signed by rookies, which calculates AAV differently from salary.
AAV-Average Annual Value AAV is the total salary of a player that counts towards the team's salary cap.
Salary Cap Total salary a team can spend on fielding its players. In 2016, the salary cap is $73 million.
